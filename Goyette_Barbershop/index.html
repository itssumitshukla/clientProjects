Our Services

Your Head

HAIR CUT | 25.00

Expertly crafted classic haircut with clippers, scissors and edgers. We then clean your neck up with your choice of trimmers or a straight razor edge up. Followed with a complete style. / 30min.

BUZZ CUT | 15.00

Keeping it simple, for you and for us. Clipper cut using one guard all around the head. Finished with a straight razor neck shave. FYI, this is not a fade. / 15min.

HEAD SHAVE | 25.00

Our signature wet shave, but for your dome. We scrub, lather, shave & sooth. Premium products used throughout. / 30min.

JUNIOR CUT | 20.00 / 25.00

Our standard cut, but for our younger folk. Junior clients must be able to sit alone on a boaster seat. (ages 4 - 12). / 30min.

SHAMPOO | 5.00

Your Face

BEARD TRIM | 15.00

Our no frills, express beard shaping service. We professionally shape your beard with clippers & shears. Finished with a straight razor (cheek line) and trimmer edge up (neck line). / 15min.

BEARD TRIM + | 25.00

Our most complete beard service for those who want a little pampering. We first sculpt your beard with clippers/scissors, followed with a series of warm & cool towel treatments, exfoliating skin treatment and a straight razor edge up. / 30min.

SHAVE | 30.00

A Crockett & Co. signature service. A classic straight razor shave with hot & cold towel service, exfoliating skin treatment, pre-shave oil, hot lather and aftershave. / 30min.
